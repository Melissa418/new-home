Are you right for Nev?

Say hello to Nev — a bright, talkative, and totally charming 8-week-old Scarlet Macaw who's looking for his forever home! He’s hand-raised, super tame, and already starting to show off his singing skills.

Nev loves attention, enjoys mimicking sounds, and is happiest when he’s hanging out with his people. He’s still a baby, so he’ll need a caring, patient home that can help him grow into the amazing companion he’s meant to be.

Is Nev right for you?

Nev’s not your average pet — he’s exotic, playful, and smart as a whip. He’s already used to being handled and loves interaction. If you’ve got the time, energy, and love to give, Nev will reward you with years of fun, chatter, and loyal friendship.

If you're ready for a feathered friend who’s full of color and personality, Nev might be your perfect match. 🦜
