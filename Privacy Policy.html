Privacy Policy

Welcome to Petco Adopt, your trusted online store for dog adoption. At Petco Adopt, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us. By accessing or using our website (petcoadopt.co), you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Introduction

At Petco Adopt, we value the trust you place in us when you provide your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our website, including those adopting pets, browsing products, or submitting inquiries. Our goal is to provide a transparent and secure experience while helping you find your perfect furry companion. This policy was last updated on June 05, 2025, and we may update it periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: When you register for an account, fill out an adoption form, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, home address, and payment details.
  • Adoption-Related Information: Details about your preferences for pet adoption, such as breed preferences, household information, and reasons for adoption.
  • Website Usage Data: Information collected automatically when you visit our site, including IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content.

We only collect information that is necessary to fulfill our services or comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To process your pet adoption applications and facilitate the adoption process.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries or comments.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience based on your feedback and usage patterns.
  • To send you updates, promotional offers, or newsletters, but only if you have opted in to receive such communications.
  • To ensure the security of our platform and prevent fraud or unauthorized access.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations, such as animal welfare and data protection laws.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share data with third-party vendors who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, or delivering adoption services (e.g., payment processors or delivery partners).
  • With Adoption Partners: Information may be shared with shelters, rescue organizations, or veterinarians involved in the adoption process to ensure a safe and suitable match.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Petco Adopt or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to this Privacy Policy.

We implement strict confidentiality agreements and data protection measures with all third parties to safeguard your information.

5. Data Security

Protecting your data is a top priority at Petco Adopt. We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online transmission or storage method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a data breach occurs, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to the data we hold about you and request corrections if it is inaccurate.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us directly.
  • Restriction: You can request that we restrict the processing of your data under certain conditions.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@petcoadopt.co. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences and login details.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve our services.
  • Deliver personalized content and advertisements.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our website.

8. Children’s Privacy

Petco Adopt is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it and notify the child’s parent or guardian. Parents or guardians should contact us at privacy@petcoadopt.co if they believe their child has provided information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your region, including those that may have different data protection laws. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection regulations, such as the use of standard contractual clauses or other legal mechanisms to protect your data.

10. Retention of Your Information

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including providing services, ensuring legal compliance, and resolving disputes. Adoption records may be kept for a minimum of five years to comply with animal welfare regulations, after which they will be securely deleted or anonymized.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy
